ABOUT US

Founded in March 2015 by Sanchita Singh Roy, Brand "Saanchi by Sanchita" under "Prapti Enterprises" has become a respected, recognised and revered name in a short span of time. The company owns, creates and operates distinctive label catering to distinct segments. Saanchi by Sanchita creates classic, free spirited silhouettes from Western influence blended with Indian embroideries, textiles and styling apt for the young at heart and in age.

Today, Saanchi by Sanchita has a widespread reach that extends not just across the country, but beyond the borders as well. Within India, the network includes well above twenty points of sale with one exclusive brand store in the fashion capital and over nineteen multi-brand large format stores in more than fifteen cities. Globallv. Saanchi by sanchita marked its presence in Asian Market.
